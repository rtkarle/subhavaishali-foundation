Career Guidance & Social Responsibility Session

Motivating students to build a purposeful career and contribute to society.

About This Activity

On 14 August 2024, a guest lecture was conducted at RBNB College, Shrirampur. The session focused on guiding students about career planning, building the right mindset, and understanding their responsibilities towards society.

Students were introduced to various career paths, decision-making strategies, and the importance of developing discipline, ethics, and awareness in their lives. The goal of this session was to encourage students to grow as responsible and value-driven citizens.
