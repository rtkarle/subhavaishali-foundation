Guest Lecture Session

Guiding students towards clarity, discipline, and future goals.

About This Activity

On 26 July 2024, a guest lecture was organized at Maharshi Dayanand College, Parel (Mumbai). The session aimed at providing students with meaningful guidance on career development, communication skills, goal-setting, and personal growth.

The lecture encouraged young learners to develop discipline, clarity of vision, and the motivation needed to progress in their academic and professional lives. Through an interactive discussion, students received insights on how to build confidence, take responsibility, and prepare for future opportunities.
