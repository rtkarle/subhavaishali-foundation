Why It Matters
Your Help Goes Where It Truly Counts
Many of the children and families we work with are fighting silent battles: no proper schooling, poor health, lack of food and zero support systems.

Your contribution does more than meet basic needs – it restores self-respect, opens new opportunities and reminds them that they are not alone. Even a small, consistent support can transform the path of a child’s life and uplift an entire family.
Trust & Accountability
Our Transparency Promise
✔ Every donation is recorded & verified: your transaction ID and payment screenshot help us confirm each contribution before it is logged in our records.

✔ Funds used only for welfare: we do not divert donations for unrelated expenses; everything goes into education, health, environment or community programs run under Subhvaishali Foundation.

✔ Documentation on request: you can write to us any time to understand how your support is being utilised, which activities it has fuelled and what impact it has created on ground.
