Free Medical Camp

Serving tribal families with essential healthcare & medical support.

About This Activity

Subhvaishali Foundation participated as a volunteer team in the three-day free medical camp organized by Sevankur Bharat (Konkan Province). During this outreach, our volunteers offered essential medical checkups, basic treatments, primary consultations, and health awareness support for tribal and rural families in remote villages.

The goal of this camp was to provide accessible healthcare to regions where medical facilities are limited, conduct screenings, guide patients on their health conditions, and offer immediate support to those in need. These moments reflect the compassion, dedication, and service spirit of our team.
