Student Interaction Session

Encouraging students to develop confidence, clarity, and positive mindset.

About This Activity

On 24 July 2024, a Student Interaction Session was held at Maharshi Karve College, Parel (Mumbai). The goal of the session was to help students gain clarity about personal development, learning attitude, career mindset, and responsibilities.

Students openly discussed their doubts and challenges regarding studies, future planning, and confidence-building. The session motivated them to stay focused, improve communication, and build a disciplined approach towards their goals.
