Free Health Camp — Phanspada

A three-day medical outreach for tribal families in Palghar district.

About This Activity

On 26 September 2024, under the initiative “Three Days for the Nation” organized by Sevankur Bharat – Konkan Prant, volunteers visited Phanspada in Palghar district. The purpose was to understand the lifestyle, social challenges, economic conditions, and health issues faced by the tribal community.

Following this village interaction, a three-day free medical camp was organized to support the local residents. The camp focused on basic medical checkups, guidence, awareness sessions, and addressing immediate health concerns.

A volunteer from Subhvaishali Foundation actively participated in this event, providing service and supporting the medical team during the camp.

Date

🗓 26 September 2024

Location

📍 Phanspada, Jawhar Taluka, Palghar District

Highlights
